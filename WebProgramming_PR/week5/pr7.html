IEEE 802.11ac and IEEE 802.11ax

Brief introduction

IEEE 802.11ac

IEEE 802.11ac is an amendment to IEEE 802.11, published in December 2013, that build on 802.11n. The 802.ac standard was retroactively labelled as Wi-Fi 5 by the Wi-Fi alliance.


IEEE 802.11ax

IEEE 802.11ax si the successor to 802.11ac, marketed as Wi-Fi 6 (2.4GHz and 5GHz) by the Wi-Fi Alliance. It is also known as High Efficiency Wi-Fi, for the overall improverments to Wi-Fi 6 clients under dense environments.
